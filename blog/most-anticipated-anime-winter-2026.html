Most Anticipated Anime of Winter 2026: The Season That Could Redefine the Year

By John Paul Pajaro January 14, 2026
WINTER 2026 ANIME

Winter 2026 is shaping up to be one of the strongest anime seasons in recent memory. With major sequels, highly anticipated originals, and several long-awaited adaptations, anime fans are spoiled for choice. Here's the definitive list of the most anticipated titles dropping between January and March 2026 — ranked by hype, production quality, and potential to dominate the year.

#1. Solo Leveling Season 2: Arise from the Shadow

After Season 1 became a global phenomenon, A-1 Pictures returns with Sung Jin-woo's next arc. Expect bigger fights, deeper lore, and jaw-dropping animation. The manhwa's massive fanbase is ready — this could be the biggest anime event of 2026.

#2. Dandadan Season 2

Science SARU continues their insane adaptation of Yukinobu Tatsu's wild manga. Season 1 set the bar impossibly high with its chaotic energy, stunning visuals, and perfect comedy-horror balance. Season 2 is expected to go even crazier.

#3. Chainsaw Man: The Movie (Reze Arc)

MAPPA finally brings the beloved Reze Arc to the big screen. Tatsuki Fujimoto's explosive storytelling + MAPPA's signature fluid action = guaranteed hype. This one will likely dominate theaters and social media.

#4. Blue Lock Season 2

The U-20 Japan Arc is coming. More intense soccer battles, insane character development, and that signature Blue Lock ego energy. Season 1 was a breakout hit — Season 2 could turn this into a full-blown sports anime classic.

#5. Kaiju No. 8 Season 2

Production I.G returns with Kafka Hibino's next chapter. Bigger kaiju, deeper secrets, and more stunning fight choreography. The first season was a monster (pun intended) hit — Season 2 will likely go even harder.

#6. My Hero Academia: Final Season (Part 2)

The final battle is here. All Might's legacy, Deku's growth, and the war against All For One reach their climax. Bones is going all out — expect emotional highs and insane animation.

#7. Sakamoto Days

Netflix + TMS Entertainment bring Yuto Suzuki's hit manga to life. Stylish action, hilarious comedy, and a retired assassin trying to live a normal life. Early trailers look insane — this could be the surprise hit of the season.

#8. Wind Breaker Season 2

CloverWorks continues the delinquent high-school action with more gang battles, character development, and stylish animation. Season 1 was a sleeper hit — Season 2 is expected to build on that momentum.

#9. The Apothecary Diaries Season 2

Maomao and Jinshi return in the palace intrigue mystery that became one of 2024's biggest surprises. More poison, more drama, more perfect character chemistry. This is appointment viewing.

#10. Dan Da Dan Season 2 (Tentative)

If the schedule holds, this wild supernatural comedy-action series will return with more chaos, ghosts, aliens, and the best animation in the game. Season 1 was a breakout — Season 2 could be legendary.
