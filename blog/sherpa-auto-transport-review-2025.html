Overview of Sherpa Auto Transport

Founded in 2017 in Charlotte, North Carolina, Sherpa Auto Transport is a brokerage that connects customers with a network of over 13,000 vetted carriers. Unlike direct carriers, Sherpa doesn’t own its fleet but carefully selects drivers with strong safety records, requiring at least $1 million in liability coverage and $100,000 in cargo insurance. This approach allows them to offer competitive pricing and flexibility, which I appreciated when booking my $950 shipment.

Sherpa specializes in door-to-door shipping for cars, SUVs, trucks, vans, and motorcycles across all 50 U.S. states, including Hawaii and Alaska (though Hawaii shipments are paused until October 2025). Their standout feature is the Price Lock Promise, which guarantees your quote with up to $300 to cover any price increases—a rarity in an industry known for bait-and-switch tactics. I found this transparency refreshing, as my final cost matched the initial $950 quote exactly.

They offer both open and enclosed transport options. I chose open transport to save $400, but a friend shipping a classic Mustang opted for enclosed transport at $1,500 to protect it from weather. Sherpa also provides expedited pickup for an additional fee, ideal for tight schedules, and a Clean Car Guarantee, reimbursing up to $20 for a post-delivery car wash. These perks made my experience feel personalized and hassle-free.
