Nina Dobrev in 2026: Where Is She Now & What’s Next for the Vampire Diaries Star?

By John Paul Pajaro January 11, 2026
NINA DOBREV

It’s been over a decade since Nina Dobrev became a household name as Elena Gilbert in The Vampire Diaries, but the Bulgarian-Canadian actress is still very much a force in Hollywood. As we enter 2026, fans are asking: Where is she now, what has she been up to lately, and what’s on the horizon? Let’s catch up on her journey since Mystic Falls and look ahead at what’s next.

Life After Vampire Diaries

After leaving The Vampire Diaries in 2015 (with a brief return in the finale), Nina transitioned into a more diverse career. She starred in the action-thriller xXx: Return of Xander Cage (2017) alongside Vin Diesel, the romantic comedy Love Hard (2021) on Netflix, and the psychological thriller The Final Girls (2015). She also became a producer with her company, Dobrev Productions, focusing on female-led stories.

Recent Projects (2024-2025)

Nina has kept a relatively low profile in the last couple of years, but she’s been active in wellness and lifestyle. She launched Feeling Good, her podcast where she talks mental health, fitness, and personal growth. She also starred in the indie thriller Trigger Warning (2024) opposite Jessica Alba, and had a recurring role in the Hulu series Death and Other Details (2025). Her most recent on-screen appearance was a cameo in a comedy special, showing she’s still comfortable dipping back into lighter roles.

Personal Life & Wellness Focus

In 2026, Nina is more open than ever about mental health and self-care. She’s been dating Shaun White since 2020 (they got engaged in 2024), and the couple often shares glimpses of their life together on social media. Nina’s fitness journey — including boxing, yoga, and healthy eating — has inspired many fans. She’s become a vocal advocate for mental health awareness and body positivity.

What’s Next in 2026?

Rumors are swirling about a potential return to the big screen in a major action or thriller role. There’s also talk of a Vampire Diaries reboot or spin-off, though nothing is confirmed. Nina has teased a new project with a female director that she’s producing herself. Given her track record of choosing meaningful roles, whatever she does next will likely be personal and impactful.

Personal opinion: Nina Dobrev has quietly built one of the most graceful post-fame careers in Hollywood. She’s not chasing the spotlight — she’s creating her own path. Whether she returns to genre TV, leads a blockbuster, or keeps producing, she’s one of the most authentic voices in entertainment today.
